From Activity-Obsessed to Judgment-Focused

The Problem

Team was hired for hustle. "I can do 100 calls a day." Activity metrics were through the roof. But AEs were drowning in unqualified meetings. Meeting-to-opportunity conversion was 18%. Turnover was 65% annually. The VP of Sales was frustrated: "We have high activity but terrible pipeline quality."

The Diagnosis

Free SDR OS Audit revealed:

  • Targeting Engine: 28/100 (ICP was "anyone in tech")
  • Gatekeeper: 15/100 (no filters before meetings booked)
  • Consistency Layer: 42/100 (no standard playbook)
  • Diagnostic Cadence: 10/100 (problems found at end of quarter)
  • Signal Tower: 22/100 (no early warning system)

Root cause: Not process. People architecture. Hired for activity. Paid for meetings. No rhythm to catch problems early.

The Intervention

Layer 1: People Architecture Rebuild

  • Redesigned hiring profile (judgment-focused, not activity-focused)
  • Shifted compensation from $50/meeting to $300/qualified SQL
  • Implemented 90-day transition period

Layer 2: Operating System Installation

  • Targeting Engine: Unified 3 separate ICP lists into 1 account list with scoring
  • Gatekeeper: Added Problem Likelihood filter (AI-based) + Role Accessibility check
  • Consistency Layer: Built SDR playbook (same steps, every rep, every time)
  • Diagnostic Cadence: Installed Monday-Friday diagnostic rhythm
  • Signal Tower: Built 4-signal dashboard (updated weekly)

Burnout Spiral to Sustainable System

The Problem

High activity. High burnout. Team was optimized for "make more calls." SDRs were doing 80+ calls daily. Turnover hit 72%. When people left, their pipeline disappeared. One strong performer carried the team, and everyone knew it.

The Diagnosis

The Audit revealed:

  • Hiring: Optimized for "hustle" (wrong profile)
  • Compensation: $60/meeting + bonus for activity (wrong incentive)
  • Consistency: 15 different approaches to prospecting (no standard)
  • Diagnostic: No early rhythm (problems at forecast)
  • Signal: No visibility into what was actually happening

Root cause: Everything was backwards. Activity-obsessed culture disguised system failure as people failure.

The Intervention

Complete Rebuild

  • Shifted hiring from "activity grinders" to "judgment-focused" reps
  • Changed compensation from activity to quality (meetings that convert)
  • Built Targeting Engine (eliminated low-fit prospecting)
  • Installed Consistency Layer (everyone follows same playbook)
  • Added Diagnostic Cadence (catch problems Monday, not Friday)
  • Implemented Signal Tower (leading indicators, weekly review)
